General info

We are currently planning to hold meetings once every two weeks. We have yet to decide a meeting place, but when decided, we will update this website and alert members through classroom and instagram. Caito and Eric will stay at blocs for 3-4 hours during meetings. Members are free to come and go as they please. We will also be holding bouldering workshops during some meetings to help members improve.

Transportation

We are going to be bussing from Harry Ainlay to blocs. If you have transportation of your own, feel free to drive yourself and your friends there! In the cold winter months, we may have to cancel meetups if the weather gets dangerously cold.
