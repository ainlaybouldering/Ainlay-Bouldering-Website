General info

We are currently planning to hold meetings once every two weeks. We will meet outside of exit 17, near the outdoor classroom. We will be leaving for Blocs at 3:15. Caito and Eric will stay at Blocs until around 6:30 pm, at which point we will go home. Members are free to come and go as they please. We will also be holding bouldering workshops during some meetings to help members improve.

Schedule

Down below is the tentative schedule for the first few months. We will be updating this section as we finalize dates.

Location

We will be going to Blocs bouldering gym, located at 8761 51 Ave NW, Edmonton, AB T6E 5H1. Blocs is a bouldering-only gym, meaning there are no ropes or harnesses. This makes it perfect for beginners and experienced climbers alike. The gym has a wide variety of problems ranging from very easy to extremely difficult. There is also a small weight room and a yoga studio.

Transportation

We are going to be bussing from Harry Ainlay to blocs. If you have transportation of your own, feel free to drive yourself and your friends there! In the cold winter months, we may have to cancel meetups if the weather gets dangerously cold. You will, unfortunately, have to arrange your own ride home, as transporation is too expensive.

Costs

We are currently working on getting a group discount for members of the club. The regular price for bouldering at Blocs is $22 including rental shoes. We will update this section when we have more information.
