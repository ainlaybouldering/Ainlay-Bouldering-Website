General Information

We plan to hold competitions with small prizes(e.g rolls of tape, one free entry pass, instant noodles, drinks) semi-regularly. Every three months, we will be holding a competition with a medium prize(around $50). At the end of the year, we will be holding a large competition with a raffle prize of climbing shoes worth over 200 dollars and other smaller prizes for the winners. All competitions will be cost free to enter and open to all members of the club.

More information will be released in the future. The first competition will be held sometime near the end of October.

Structure

Tentative Rules

  • All climbs must be completed within the competition time frame.
  • Participants must adhere to all gym rules and regulations.
  • The competition will be split into four levels
    1. Can do red tags and some orange tag climbs in one session
    2. Can do orange tags and some yellow tags in one session
    3. Can do yellow tags and some green tags in one session
    4. Can do green tags and above in one session
  • Unsportsmanlike conduct will result in disqualification.
  • Climbs sent are tracked by yourself. Be honest. If we catch you cheating, you will be disqualified
