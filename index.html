About us

We are a student-run club dedicated to expanding Edmonton's climbing community. At Ainlay Bouldering Club, we believe climbing is for everyone, and we strive to create an inclusive nurturing environment for climbers of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner looking to try climbing for the first time or an experienced climber seeking to push your limits with likeminded individuals, we invite you to join us on this exciting journey. Together, let's reach new heights and build lasting friendships along the way!
